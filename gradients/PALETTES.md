## Groupings by value

### Dark - app-chrome backgrounds
Dominantly dark fields that can sit behind UI chrome without fighting foreground content:

- **gradient(6)** - Saki-nezu `#475B5C` / Susuiro `#4B3F36`: entirely low-value, near-monochrome; the safest chrome candidate.
- **gradient(4)** - Nakabeni `#C93756` to Karacha-aki `#785A38` to Karasuba `#180614`: deep crow-black anchor; use the lower two-thirds.
- **gradient(18)** - Shikkoku `#0C0C0C` / Naka-nezu `#283030` (to Usubeni `#F2666C`): black for most of the frame with a single vivid base accent.
- **gradient(1)** - Kuwanomi-iro `#5C2A3D` field with the Wasurenagusa `#89C3EB` glow: dark chrome with a built-in focal highlight.
- **gradient(20)** - Mokutan `#250E0E` upper half: ebony-dominant; crop above the willow-green base for chrome use.

Also note: the top stops of the full-range linears - Shinai `#243850` (7), Kuro `#1C1C1C` (10), Kachi-tetsu `#2F3640` (11), Yoai `#101E30` (14), Sumimai `#101C28` (23) - are chrome-grade dark colors on their own, even though those gradients run to light.

### Mid - atmospheric (marketing/presentation pages)
Mid-value or full-range sweeps; too much tonal travel for chrome, ideal as hero/backdrop art:

- **gradient(3)** (silk/navy/mouse-grey fog), **gradient(5)** (ice glow with amber), **gradient(16)** (ink to summer water to blush), **gradient(19)** (coal to lead to washed persimmon), **gradient(21)** (sappanwood to ultramarine to turquoise), **gradient(22)** (shrimp brown to aubergine to budding green).
- The full-range dark-to-light verticals: **gradient(7), (8), (10), (11), (12), (13), (14), (15), (23)** - classic sky-like sweeps; strong for section backgrounds where text color can switch across the fold.

### Light
High-value overall; suited to light-mode surfaces and airy pages:

- **gradient(2)** - White Tea Grey `#B8B0A6` with a Verdigris `#48929B` bloom; the quietest of the set.
- **gradient(17)** - Usubeni `#F2666C` to Daidai `#F39800` to Nyūhaku `#F3F3F3`: light but vivid; sunrise energy.
- **gradient(9)** - mid-light with the Hiwa-iro `#D7E82C` acid band; light with a jolt.
