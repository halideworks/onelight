Logo explorations

Ten directions for the Onelight mark. The register is the name itself: one-light dailies, a single printer light passed over the picture. Every card shows the mark at identity size, at 32/24/16, inside a browser tab, and locked up with the wordmark in Space Grotesk. Judge at 16px first; a mark that dies in the tab dies everywhere.

1. The one light

A single source above a horizon. The most reduced possible telling of the name: one light, the work below it. Reads at any size; almost typographic.
